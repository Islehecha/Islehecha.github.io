I am a Ph.D. student in Control Science and Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology and Peng Cheng Laboratory, advised by Prof. Zhiyun Lin. My current research focuses on generative ergodic search for dynamic target distributions and robust motion control.

My doctoral work builds hierarchical closed-loop architectures for dynamic target search, combining diffusion-model-based generative planning with reinforcement-learning-based simplex robust control. The goal is to improve time-varying target search under severe physical disturbances while maintaining consistent planning and control behavior.

Research Interests
======
* Generative planning for dynamic target search
* Ergodic exploration and information-theoretic search
* Robust motion control under physical disturbances
* Adaptive finite-time control for stochastic nonlinear systems
* Robot manipulator tracking and multi-agent coordination
